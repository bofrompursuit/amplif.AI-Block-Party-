Inclusive by Design

Everyone is Welcome 🤝

BlockFest is built so every neighbor — regardless of age or ability — can fully participate and enjoy the party.

♿

Mobility Friendly

Designate accessible zones, marked paths, and reserved parking right in your blueprint.

  • Accessible parking spots
  • Smooth vendor lane paths
  • Wheelchair-priority seating
👶

Kids Zone

Dedicated safe activity areas placed away from traffic, with kid-friendly schedule slots.

  • Bounce house / play area
  • Face painting corner
  • Stroller parking
🦻

Hearing Support

Signal accommodations during sign-up so organizers can arrange ASL and visual cues.

  • ASL interpreter requests
  • Visual announcement boards
  • Quiet relaxation zone
👴

Senior Comfort

Shaded seating, scheduled rest breaks in the AI timetable, and easy-access food zones.

  • Shaded rest areas
  • Hydration stations
  • Gentle activity scheduling
🌈

Sensory Friendly

Mark quiet zones in your layout. The AI planner avoids loud events near quiet spaces.

  • Quiet zones on map
  • Sensory break scheduling
  • Low-stimulation rest area
⛑️

Safety First

First-aid station placement in blueprint, permit tracking, and safety check announcements in the schedule.

  • First aid station marker
  • Emergency path planning
  • Permit deadline reminders
